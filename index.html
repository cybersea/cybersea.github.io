Swan Lake Bathymetry and Fish Survey

The source data were collected from a small boat using a Biosonics single-beam echosounder.

The bathymetry data points were converted into a bathymetry surface model and depth contour lines were generated from this surface model. Fish were also detected using the echosounder and their surveyed locations are shown on the map.

  • Learn more about Biosonics

Uninsured Americans in 2015 by US County

This map was created using a sample lesson for New Maps Plus, Map 671 course on integrating QGIS with CartoDB.

The lesson involved reformatting, converting and joining some tabular data with a shapefile of US Counties in QGIS. These data were uploaded and styled into a chloropleth map in CartoDB.

  • Interactive Map: Uninsured Americans in 2015 by US County
  • Data Source: County Health Rankings & Roadmaps
  • Make your own map with QGIS and CartoDB: New Maps Plus Map 671 Module 08

Power Generation Proportional Symbol Map

This map was created with custom JavaScript code using the Leaflet JavaScript Library. Power plant data were filtered and displayed with proportional symbols to contrast power generation capacity of three energy sources, Nuclear, Solar, and Wind.

Users of the interactive map can click on an area to display only plants within 500 km of that location. Popups display additional detail about the individual plants and summarize power generation capacity within the focus area.

  • See the map: Power Generation Proportional Symbol Map
  • Data Source: Department of Energy EIA Form 923
